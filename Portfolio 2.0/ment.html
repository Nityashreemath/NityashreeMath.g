Mentcare is a app curated for children to take care and track their mental health, with the feature to share their problems with the counselor and write down their thoughts, iterated after a elaborate discussion with a psychology student.

My Role:  Lead UI/UX designer
Duration:   2 months
Tools used:   Figma, Midjourney

Design Process:







User Personas:







Problem Statement:



There are many issues children face, but are often neglected and not addressed properly which might be the root cause problems like depression, insomnia etc in the future.





Challenges



1. Make children feel heard
2. Keep a track of their mental well being
3. A counselor for children for addressing their problems





User Reasearch Summary



I thought about common mental health problems among children and realized they faced small issues which are usually neglected sometimes children just wanted to vent out.
The feedback received made it clear that some students needed consultation while some just needed guidance and some just needed to vent out so combining all the data the project was made.





Competitive Analysis







How is medcare different?



1. Mentcare is for children
2. Free of cost
3. Has health tracker
4. Children can vent out





Ideation:






I did a quick ideation exercise to come up with ideas for how to combine all the required things for mental well being of children
