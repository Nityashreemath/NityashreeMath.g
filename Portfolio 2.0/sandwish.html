Sandwish is a sandwich delivery app for a imaginary company which sells customizable sandwiches, the users can choose from the given variety or customize their own..

My Role:  Lead UI/UX designer
Duration:   2 weeks
Tools used:   Figma, Pinterest

Design Process:







Problem statement :



People who are allergic to certain ingredients cannot freely eat precooked food , but this becomes difficult for working people as they don’t have enough time to cook

>



Responsiblities :



Conducting interviews, paper and digital wire-framing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.

User Reasearch Summary



I thought about common mental health problems among children and realized they faced small issues which are usually neglected sometimes children just wanted to vent out.
The feedback received made it clear that some students needed consultation while some just needed guidance and some just needed to vent out so combining all the data the project was made.





Ideation:






As the initial design phase continued, I made sure to base screen designs on feedback and findings from  the user research.
