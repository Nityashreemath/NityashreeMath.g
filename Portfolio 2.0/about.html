As an Engineering student, I have always had an interest in the intersection of technology and creativity. While working on a group project, I discovered UI/UX designing, and it immediately resonated with me. I am passionate about creating designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional, and I believe that user-centered design is the key to achieving this goal.


As a good listener, I try to understand the needs and problems of the end-users and empathize with their issues. I then use this understanding to brainstorm different ideas and come up with solutions that can improve their overall user experience. I am also proficient in various design tools like Adobe XD, and Figma and have experience in coding languages such as HTML and CSS.


Ultimately, my goal is to pursue a career in UI/UX design and make a positive impact on people's lives through design. I am excited to continue exploring this field and expanding my knowledge and skills through internships, professional development opportunities, and other experiences.
